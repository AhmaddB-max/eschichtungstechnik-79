Getting Here

Everything you need to know before your visit

Parking

Street parking available on 8th St and surrounding streets. Additional lot parking nearby. Please check signs for time limits.

Public Transit

Accessible via MTS bus routes. Check the MTS website for schedules and route planning. We're walkable from several major stops.

Bike Friendly

Bike racks available out front. South Bay's getting more bike-friendly every day. Ride over and enjoy the journey!

What to Expect

Warm, Welcoming Space

Come as you are. Whether you're here for a quick coffee or staying all afternoon, you're home.

Indoor & Outdoor Seating

Cozy indoor space with vinyl listening area, plus outdoor seating for those perfect San Diego days.

Community Vibes

You'll likely meet a neighbor, make a new friend, or reconnect with an old one. That's the magic of FoF.

Family Friendly

Kids welcome! We're a gathering place for the whole family and community.

See You Soon!

Can't wait to welcome you to our space. First time? Ask for Marco, Brandon, or Aaron — they'd love to meet you.
