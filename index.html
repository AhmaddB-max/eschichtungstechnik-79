Our Story

More Than Just Coffee

We're serving the best that we can create while being reminded of who we are and where we come from.

Friends of Friends is a warm, nostalgic gathering place inspired by flavors of home, a friend's house, or walking home from school. Founded by Marco Laguna, chef Brandon James-Keeper, and Aaron Henderson, we're building a safe, creative space for South Bay creatives, families, and friends to connect over specialty coffee, incredible food, and vinyl records.
