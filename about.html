Our Story

Where Friends Become Family

We're serving the best that we can create while being reminded of who we are and where we come from.

More Than a Coffee Shop

Friends of Friends is a warm, nostalgic gathering place inspired by flavors of home, a friend's house, or walking home from school. We blend specialty coffee, incredible food, beer & wine, and vinyl records to create a safe, creative space for South Bay to connect, create, and celebrate.

Community First

We're your neighbors, your friends, your gathering place. Every person who walks through our door matters.

Flavors of Home

Filipino and Hispanic-inspired dishes that remind us of where we're from and celebrate our heritage.

Music & Culture

Vinyl listening sessions, record exchanges, and DJ sets that bring people together through sound.

Southbay All Day

Proud National City Roots

Located in the heart of National City at 127 E 8th St, we're deeply connected to our South Bay community. This isn't just where we work—it's where we live, where we grew up, and where our families call home.

Every day, we serve our neighbors, celebrate local artists, and create a space where South Bay creatives, families, and friends can gather. We're not just supporting the community—we ARE the community.

What We Believe

Quality Over Everything

From sourcing the best beans to perfecting every burger, we never compromise on quality. Our community deserves the best.

Authenticity Always

We stay true to our roots, our culture, and our values. What you see is what you get—real people, real food, real connections.

Inclusive Space

Everyone is welcome here. We're building a safe, creative gathering place for all people, backgrounds, and stories.

Supporting Local

We partner with local artists, musicians, and creators to showcase South Bay talent and keep our community thriving.
